ARCHIWORX is a dynamic architect office located in central Bucharest.
Architecture solutions are created through dialogue, exploration and sustainability. With us process starts with creating a common ground so together we can start the exciting and rich journey towards a unique project.

For each architecture project, interior design or furniture design, Roxana collaborates with a network of professionals in various fields to solve the project tasks.
We create a playful, bold and contextual architecture.
